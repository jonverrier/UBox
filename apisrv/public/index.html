UltraBox - virtual tools for coaches.

Your gym is differentiated by community, and by getting results. UltraBox builds tools to help you grow and engage your community, and to measure and promote your results.

Cohort programs

Run cohort coaching blocks. A coaching block has a specific set of members, a schedule, and has specific measures to track development over time - such as maximum lifts, or fastest workouts. Use cohorts to deliver proof that you develop your members capabilities. You can analyse your cohorts, and compare to cohorts from other gyms, to find areas for improvement.

Throwdowns

Run competitions where members complete the same workout, running through heats to a final. Individually, or in groups - like the CrossFit Games, but for your gym, or for you to run in partership with other gyms.

Focus

UltraBox is laser focussed on supporting coached workouts. We believe people develop best when coached, and want to help you grow a more successful business.
